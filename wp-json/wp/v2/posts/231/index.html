No one likes cavities. They can cause pain, discomfort, and lead to other problems such as tooth loss and gum disease. However, luckily there is a way to treat cavities and prevent them from ever forming. With tooth decay thriving with all the sugary sweets and drinks within our grasp, it\u2019s easy to forget proper oral hygiene. That is why were are here to remind you the dangers of cavities and how to avoid them. <\/p>\n

Tooth decay occurs when the hard, outer layer of your teeth, known as your tooth enamel, begins to wear down and deteriorate because of the constant attacks from oral bacteria in your mouth. After a while, your enamel weakens and cavities are born. If cavities are left untreated, tooth decay can result in serious oral complications. <\/p>\n

However, there is no reason to fear when you learn how to protect your smile from tooth decay! By following simple steps, you can keep your smile healthy. These tips include: <\/p>\n

\u2022\tBrush your teeth twice a day
\n\u2022\tFlossing daily
\n\u2022\tUse effective Mouthwash
\n\u2022\tVisit your dentist regularly for a cleaning
\n\u2022\tReplace your toothbrush regularly<\/p>\n
