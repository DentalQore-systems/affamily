Dr. Clayton Hansen has been practicing dentistry in American Fork, Utah, since 1998. After achieving his bachelor\u2019s degree from Utah State University in Logan, Dr. Hansen went on to dental school at the University of Texas Health Science Center San Antonio, graduating in 1997. In order to best serve his patients, he continues his education multiple times each year to stay current on the newest technology and trends in dental healthcare. Dr. Hansen is a member of the American Dental Association (ADA), Utah Dental Association (UDA), and Academy of General Dentistry (AGD). He also serves on the Dental Advisory Board at Mountainland Applied Technology College (MATC) in Orem, Utah.<\/p>\n

Dr. Hansen enjoys his job and strives to help his patients have a healthy, happy smile and the confidence that comes with it. In his free time, he enjoys skiing and spending time with his family.<\/p>\n
