Your oral health is important to your dentist, Dr. Clayton M. Hansen, and our dental team, which is why we offer strong, successful, and reliable dental restorations at American Fork Family Dentist.<\/span><\/p>\n

One beneficial restoration we offer at our office is a dental crown, which is a great solution if you need to restore an unhealthy tooth and improve your smile\u2019s appearance. There are many situations when a dental crown can help you, including when:<\/span><\/p>\n

-You need to keep a weak tooth from breaking, or when you need to hold a cracked tooth together.<\/span><\/p>\n

-You need to restore a broken tooth, or when you need to repair a tooth that has been severely worn down.<\/span><\/p>\n

-You need to support a tooth that has a large filling because there\u2019s not a lot of tooth structure left.<\/span><\/p>\n

-You need to hold a dental bridge in its place.<\/span><\/p>\n

-You need to disguise a tooth that is extremely misshapen or discolored.<\/span><\/p>\n

-You need to cover a dental implant.<\/span><\/p>\n

-You need to improve the appearance of your smile.<\/span><\/p>\n
