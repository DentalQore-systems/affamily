Dental flaws can lower your self-esteem and confidence. When a patient wishes for an attractive smile that will boost their confidence, Dr. Clayton M. Hansen may recommend cosmetic dentistry in American Fork, Utah. Cosmetic dentistry includes a range of services whose purpose is to enhance and restore the beauty of your smile. Cosmetic dentistry can repair dental imperfections including:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Chipped, cracked, or broken teeth<\/li>\n
  • Worn down teeth<\/li>\n
  • Slightly crooked or crowded teeth<\/li>\n
  • Discolored teeth<\/li>\n
  • Misshaped teeth<\/li>\n
  • Gaps or spaces between teeth<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Before recommending any cosmetic dental treatment, our dentist will hold a cosmetic consultation with you. During this appointment, your smile goals and dreams will be discussed. After our dentist understands what you wish to accomplish, he will examine the current condition of your smile. Then our dentist will recommend one or more cosmetic treatment that will help you reach the look you desire. Our dentist and dental team will ensure that you understand your treatment options so that you can make an informed decision. Together, you and our dentist will form a treatment plan.<\/p>\n

    We welcome you to contact our dental office today to learn more and to schedule a consultation.
