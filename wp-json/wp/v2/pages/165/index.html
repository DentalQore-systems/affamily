If your tooth needs its shape, size, strength, or appearance restored, Dr. Clayton M. Hansen may recommend a dental crown in American Fork, Utah. A dental crown is a tooth-shaped covering that is cemented over your tooth, covering the entire visible portion of your tooth. Our dentist may recommend a dental crown to:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Protect a weak tooth from breaking<\/li>\n
  • Hold a cracked tooth together<\/li>\n
  • Restore a broken or worn down tooth<\/li>\n
  • Cover and support a tooth with a large dental filling<\/li>\n
  • Anchor a dental bridge<\/li>\n
  • Cover irregularly shaped teeth<\/li>\n
  • Cover badly stained teeth<\/li>\n
  • Cover a dental implant<\/li>\n
  • Improve a tooth\u2019s appearance<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Our dentist may place a dental crown on primary (baby) teeth to:<\/p>\n

      \n
    • Save a tooth that is too badly damaged to support a dental filling<\/li>\n
    • Protect the teeth of a child that is at high risk for cavities<\/li>\n
    • Protect the teeth of a child who has trouble with oral hygiene<\/li>\n
    • Decrease the use of anesthesia on a child who is unable to cooperate during dental treatments<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

      We invite you to contact our dental office today to learn more about dental crowns and to schedule a visit with our dentist.<\/p>\n
